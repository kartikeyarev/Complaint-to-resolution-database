ER DIAGRAM DESCRIPTION
(Use this text to draw your diagram in Draw.io or Lucidchart)

1. ENTITIES AND ATTRIBUTES:

[Users]
- user_id (PK)
- full_name
- email
- role
- created_at

[Status]
- status_id (PK)
- status_name
- is_final

[State_Transitions]
- transition_id (PK)
- from_status_id (FK)
- to_status_id (FK)
- allowed_role

[Complaints]
- complaint_id (PK)
- user_id (FK)
- title
- description
- status_id (FK)
- assigned_to (FK)
- created_at
- updated_at

[Complaint_History]
- log_id (PK)
- complaint_id (FK)
- changed_by (FK)
- old_status_id (FK)
- new_status_id (FK)
- comments
- changed_at


2. RELATIONSHIPS (Lines to draw):

Users to Complaints (Creator)
- 1 User can create MANY Complaints.
- Line from Users.user_id to Complaints.user_id (1:N)

Users to Complaints (Assignee)
- 1 User (Resolver) can be assigned to MANY Complaints.
- Line from Users.user_id to Complaints.assigned_to (1:N)

Status to Complaints
- 1 Status can belong to MANY Complaints.
- Line from Status.status_id to Complaints.status_id (1:N)

Status to State_Transitions
- 1 Status can have MANY transitions (Both as FROM and TO).
- Line from Status.status_id to State_Transitions.from_status_id (1:N)
- Line from Status.status_id to State_Transitions.to_status_id (1:N)

Complaints to Complaint_History
- 1 Complaint can have MANY History Logs.
- Line from Complaints.complaint_id to Complaint_History.complaint_id (1:N)

Users to Complaint_History
- 1 User can generate MANY History Logs (by making changes).
- Line from Users.user_id to Complaint_History.changed_by (1:N)

Status to Complaint_History
- 1 Status can appear in MANY History Logs (as old or new status).
- Line from Status.status_id to Complaint_History.old_status_id (1:N)
- Line from Status.status_id to Complaint_History.new_status_id (1:N)
